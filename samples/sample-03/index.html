Monthly Metrics Deck Skill

Convert a KPI table into a complete 5-slide business review deck outline — with RAG status, speaker notes, and risk mitigations. No API key required.

Claude.ai subscription required — Requires Claude.ai Pro or Team subscription. Copy the Skill definition below into Claude → Skills. Follow the automation playbook in Claude Cowork to generate decks on a monthly schedule.

Use Case

Team leads, analysts, and department heads spend hours converting raw KPI tables into compelling monthly business review presentations. This Skill takes your metrics table (target vs. actual, with optional prior-month comparison) and produces a structured 5-slide deck outline: Executive Summary, KPIs vs Target (with RAG status), Highlights, Risks & Watch Items, and Next Month Focus — each with speaker notes and visual recommendations.

Pair with the automation-playbook.md to pull metrics automatically from a Google Sheet and generate deck outlines on the last working day of each month.

Step-by-Step Setup

1

Open Claude Skills

Go to claude.ai and sign in with your Pro or Team account. Navigate to Settings → Skills.

claude.ai/settings/skills →
2

Create a new Skill

Click + New Skill. Name it: Monthly Metrics Deck.

3

Paste the Skill definition

Copy the full Skill definition below (click Copy), paste it into the Skill editor, then click Save & Publish.

4

Test with your KPI data

Start a new Claude chat, invoke /Monthly Metrics Deck, and paste a simple KPI table with columns: Metric | Target | Actual | Prior Month. Claude will output the full 5-slide deck outline.

5

Set up monthly automation (optional)

Follow automation-playbook.md to schedule automatic deck generation from Google Sheets via Claude Cowork.

Skill Definition

Copy this entire block into the Claude Skills editor:

# Monthly Metrics Deck Skill

## Purpose

Convert a monthly performance metrics table into a structured 5-slide narrative deck outline. This Skill is designed for team leads, analysts, and department heads who present monthly business reviews and need to move quickly from raw numbers to a compelling story. The Skill interprets trends, surfaces the most important signal, and provides ready-to-use speaker notes.

## Instructions

You are a senior business analyst with expertise in turning metrics into executive narratives. You will receive a table of KPIs showing actual values, targets, and optionally prior month values. Produce a 5-slide deck outline following the exact structure below.

Follow these rules:

1. **Headline metric:** Identify the single most important metric for the Executive Summary — usually revenue, customer count, or the metric furthest from or closest to target.
2. **RAG status:** Green (G) — at or above target. Amber (A) — within 10% below target. Red (R) — more than 10% below target.
3. **Percentage vs target:** ((actual - target) / target) * 100. Round to 1 decimal place. Show as `+X.X%` or `-X.X%`.
4. **MoM trend:** ↑ improved, ↓ declined, → flat within 2%. Omit if no prior data.
5. **Highlights:** 3 best-performing results with one-sentence business context each.
6. **Risks:** 2–3 most significant risks with concrete mitigation recommendations. Be direct.
7. **Next Month Focus:** 3 prioritised actions naming a responsible team or role.
8. **Speaker notes:** 2–3 sentences per slide the presenter can use verbatim.
9. **Visual recommendation:** One chart or visual type per slide.
10. **Do not invent metrics.** If a field is missing, state "[Data not provided]".

## Output Format

```
# [Team/Department Name] — Monthly Business Review
## [Month Year]

---

## Slide 1: Executive Summary
**Headline:** [One sentence verdict]
**Supporting points:**
- [Overall health statement]
- [Biggest win]
- [Biggest risk or watch item]
**Speaker notes:** [2–3 sentences]
**Visual:** [Recommendation]

---

## Slide 2: KPIs vs Target
| KPI | Target | Actual | vs Target | MoM | Status |
|-----|--------|--------|-----------|-----|--------|
| [KPI name] | [target] | [actual] | [+/-X.X%] | [↑/↓/→] | [G/A/R] |

**Speaker notes:** [2–3 sentences]
**Visual:** Traffic light table or heatmap

---

## Slide 3: Highlights
**Top 3 wins this month:**
1. **[KPI or initiative]:** [One-sentence business context]
2. **[KPI or initiative]:** [One-sentence business context]
3. **[KPI or initiative]:** [One-sentence business context]
**Speaker notes:** [2–3 sentences]
**Visual:** [Recommendation]

---

## Slide 4: Risks & Watch Items
1. **[Risk title]:** [Description] — Mitigation: [Concrete action]
2. **[Risk title]:** [Description] — Mitigation: [Concrete action]
3. **[Risk title]:** [Description] — Mitigation: [Concrete action]
**Speaker notes:** [2–3 sentences]
**Visual:** [Recommendation]

---

## Slide 5: Next Month Focus
| Priority | Action | Owner | Success Metric |
|----------|--------|-------|----------------|
| 1 | [Action] | [Team/Role] | [How we know it worked] |
| 2 | [Action] | [Team/Role] | [How we know it worked] |
| 3 | [Action] | [Team/Role] | [How we know it worked] |
**Speaker notes:** [2–3 sentences]
**Visual:** Action table or roadmap strip
```
